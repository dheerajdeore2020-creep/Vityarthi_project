#### ***Military Canteen Management System***



###### A Python + MySQL–based automation system designed to streamline the operations of a Military Canteen.  

###### The system manages customers, products, canteen sales, invoices, and administrative operations through a structured menu-driven interface.



---



###### \##Features



###### \### Customer Management

\- Create, view, search, modify, and delete customer records  

\- Maintains updated database of defence personnel \& families



###### \### Product Management

\- Add, display, search, modify, and delete product entries  

\- Tracks pricing, discounts, purchase dates, and stock information  

\- Handles outstanding payments and due amounts



###### \### Canteen Sales Management

\- Create sales transactions  

\- Modify and delete sale entries  

\- Automatic calculation of amount and GST (5%)  

\- Generates invoices with itemized details



###### \### Reports

1. Product purchase report  

2\. Customer invoices  

3\. Administrative activity logs



---



###### \## Tech Stack



Frontend / UI: Python (menu-based CLI)  

Backend Logic: Python  

Database: MySQL  

OS Support: Windows / Linux / macOS

Testing



The system was tested with various real-case scenarios:

\- Customer creation, searching, and modification  

\- Product addition, price updates, and deletion

\- Invoice generation and GST calculations  



All modules were validated using different inputs to ensure reliable performance.
